2001: A Space Odyssey

Title:

2001: A Space Odyssey

Year:

1968

Director:

Stanley Kubrick

Writers:

Stanley Kubrick, Arthur C. Clarke

Stars:

Keir Dullea, Gary Lockwood, William Sylvester

Genres:

Science Fiction

Synopsis:

The film follows a voyage to Jupiter with the sentient computer HAL after the discovery of a mysterious black monolith affecting human evolution.

Fun Facts:

  • Stanley Kubrick initially wanted to include voice-over narration to explain some of the more obscure elements of the film, but ultimately decided against it.
  • The film's famous "Star Gate" sequence was created using a slit-scan photography technique and is often cited as one of the most visually stunning sequences in cinema history.
